About Me

After about 25 years of a successful career as an advocate, activist, researcher, and therapist devoted to assisting survivors of interpersonal violence and trauma, I made the decision to shift gears and pursue a career in full-stack web development. I am currently a student at the UT Austin Coding Bootcamp and recently completed the UT Austin Data Analytics and Visualization Bootcamp. I am thrilled that this career path encompasses everything I love most, i.e., creativity/artistry, data management and quality assurance, implementing and honing technical skills, working both independently and collaboratively with others; and bringing complex project ideas to life. I'm looking forward to discovering what this next phase of my life brings me!
